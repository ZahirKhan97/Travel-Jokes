Description

Experience the best of Dubai with our comprehensive full-day city tour from Abu Dhabi. This tour is perfect for those looking to explore the city's iconic landmarks, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture in a single day. Our expert guides will take you on a journey through Dubai's rich history and modern marvels, ensuring you get the most out of your visit.

Your adventure begins with a comfortable pick-up from your Abu Dhabi hotel, followed by a scenic drive to Dubai. Upon arrival, you'll visit the historic Al Fahidi Neighborhood, where you can explore traditional Emirati culture and architecture. Next, hop on a traditional abra boat to cross the Dubai Creek, offering picturesque views of the city's skyline.

Itinerary

  • Pick-up

  • Al Seef & Abra Boat Ride
